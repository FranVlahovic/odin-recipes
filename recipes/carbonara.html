Pasta Carbonara

Description

Savor the authentic flavors of Rome with our Traditional Pasta Carbonara,
a timeless Italian classic crafted using traditional techniques for a rich, creamy,
and delicious experience.

Ingredients

  • 200g of Spaghetti (Bucatini)
  • 75g of Pecorino Romano
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 75g of Guanciale
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water for 8-10 minutes, for fresh pasta 3-5 minutes
  2. Meanwhile, mix together the Parmesan, black pepper and egg yolks in a small bowl and set aside
  3. Cook the guanciale in a large frying pan until crispy. Once cooked, reserve the pancetta and
    the fat that has been released during cooking separately
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a ladleful of the pasta water and drain the pasta
  5. Transfer the pasta into the bowl and add reserved guanciale fat, bain marie the pasta with the mixture
  6. Mix well until creamy then add the guanciale, and a little more pasta water if necessary
  7. Serve immediately with some more freshly grated Parmesan
